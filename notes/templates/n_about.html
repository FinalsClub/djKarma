Thanks for helping spread the academic wealth!

KarmaNotes is a shared learning database. Here you can upload your college course notes, study guides, syllabi and assignments to a common collection. By sharing your knowledge on KarmaNotes, you will earn Karma Points and access to a constantly growing compendium of academic resources.

Because KarmaNotes is a non-profit project, all of our content is freely shared under Creative Commons Share-Alike license to encourage open access and academic remixing (with proper attribution). We also respect the rights of copyright holders and maintain full compliance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. Please do not upload any copyrighted files or they may be subject to removal.

Want to know more? Check out our free and open source code or email us.
